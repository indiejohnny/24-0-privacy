Privacy Policy for 24-0

Last updated: June 2026

24-0 ("the App") is developed by an independent developer. This Privacy Policy explains what information the App collects, how it is used, and your choices regarding that information.

Summary

24-0 does not require you to create an account and does not collect your name, email address, or any personal contact information. Your game progress, run history, and preferences are stored locally on your device. The App uses third-party services for advertising and premium subscription management, described below.

Information Stored On Your Device

The App stores the following information locally on your device using standard device storage (AsyncStorage). This information is NOT transmitted to us or any third party except as described in the "Third-Party Services" section below:

  • Your run history (drafted drivers, cars, eras, and season results)
  • Your leaderboard / personal best records
  • App preferences (e.g. whether you've seen onboarding, Pro status)

You can clear this data at any time by deleting and reinstalling the App, which removes all locally stored information.

Third-Party Services

Google AdMob (Advertising)

The App displays rewarded video advertisements (e.g. to grant extra draft re-spins or to start a new run) using Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers (such as your device's advertising identifier), IP address, and general device information to serve and measure advertisements, and may use this information for ad personalization where permitted.

On iOS, the App will request your permission via Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework before using your device's advertising identifier for personalized ads. If you decline, you will still see ads, but they will not be personalized based on your activity across other apps and websites.

For more information on how Google handles data, see Google's Privacy Policy and How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services.

RevenueCat (Subscription Management)

If you purchase a "Pro" subscription, the App uses RevenueCat to manage and validate your purchase with the Apple App Store. RevenueCat receives an anonymous identifier generated by the App (not linked to your name or email) along with purchase/subscription status from Apple, in order to unlock Pro features on your device. RevenueCat does not receive your payment details — these are handled entirely by Apple.

For more information, see RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.

Apple App Store

Purchases are processed by Apple via the App Store. Apple's handling of your payment information is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The App does not require account creation or collect contact information from any user.

Data Retention

Locally stored data remains on your device until you delete the App. We do not operate servers that store your personal gameplay data.

Your Choices

  • You can decline App Tracking Transparency permission on iOS at any time via Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking.
  • You can reset your advertising identifier or limit ad tracking in your device's system settings.
  • You can delete all locally stored App data by uninstalling the App.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
